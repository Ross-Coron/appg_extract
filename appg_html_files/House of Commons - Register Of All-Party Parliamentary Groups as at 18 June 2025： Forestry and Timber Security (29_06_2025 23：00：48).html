Register Of All-Party Parliamentary Groups [as at 18 June 2025] Forestry and Timber Security

Title

All-Party Parliamentary Group on Forestry and Timber Security

Purpose

The APPG promotes sustainably managed forests for jobs and economic growth, and a growing, secure timber resource for the UK wood processing sector. Our work supports rural economies, provides low-carbon timber for house-building and combats illegal logging overseas.
